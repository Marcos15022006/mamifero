The Tiger


Information about tigers Más información sobre tigres

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera.Recognizable by its orange coat with black stripes, it has a powerful, muscular body, a large head, and a long tail. Tigers are apex predators, primarily hunting ungulates like deer and wild boar. They are solitary animals, with males and females only coming together for mating. Tigers are found in a variety of habitats, including tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps across Asia.
